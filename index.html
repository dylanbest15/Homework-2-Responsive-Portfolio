About Me

My name is Dylan Best. I am originally from Massachusetts and I currently live in Pittsburgh, PA. I attended the University of Pittsburgh and graduated in 2018 with a Bachelor's degree in Psychology. I worked for two years as a Data Analyst before enrolling in the coding bootcamp at the University of New Hampshire. My current career goal is to graduate this course and become a web developer. This portfolio will be used to demonstrate my projects completed in this course and act as a professional contact point.

Aside from my professional work, I am passionate about Ultimate Frisbee. I played for Pitt Men's Ultimate for 5 years in which we won a national silver medal in 2018. I also represented the USA in the World U24 Championships in Perth, Australia. Currently, I play semi-professional and club ultimate for the city of Pittsburgh. When I'm not on the field, you can find me playing Mario Party with friends, eating ice cream, or hanging out with my cat, Milo.
